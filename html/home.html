About Me

I am a tech enthusiast and creative problem solver with a passion for crafting unique web experiences. My journey into the tech world began in 2014 when I started coding and designing websites independently. Back then, I taught myself HTML and CSS, blending them with template builders to produce one-of-a-kind web designs.

While pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Software Development, I decided to embark on a different path and served five years as an Air Traffic Control Navigation Aids Technician in the Marines. My time overseas in 2018 broadened my horizons, and I completed my contract with an honorable discharge in January 2021.

My post-military career led me to the world of semiconductors before joining Ericsson as an Equipment Engineering Technician. Here, my passion for technology reignited, and I quickly climbed the ranks, reaching my growth ceiling in under a year.

My fascination with technology runs deep, from building servers and diving into crypto mining at the age of 15 to exploring automation techniques with Python and Linux. To further my skills, I earned certifications in Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and SQL through various online platforms, and I'm committed to ongoing learning in this dynamic field.

What sets me apart is my unwavering drive for excellence and dedication, honed over years in the Marine Corps. I'm no stranger to recognition, having been acknowledged as a top-performer both physically and academically, as well as one of the youngest Subject Matter Experts in my field. I thrive on seizing every opportunity for growth and have been entrusted with leadership roles at a young age, mentoring and educating my peers and superiors alike.

In my pursuit of mastery, I harness every available resource. Whether I'm conducting exhaustive research or leveraging AI to refine my work, I strive to be the best, aiming for recognition as a top-tier employee by future employers.

Beyond my tech prowess, I'm a self-taught musician who can play a multitude of instruments by ear, and an internationally published photographer with works featured in magazines and museums worldwide. My keen eye for detail and unique artistic perspective allow me to produce custom content and images that resonate with clients.

I'm thrilled to bring this passion, dedication, and unique skill set to the tech industry. I'm open to opportunities that allow me to collaborate, innovate, and excel. Let's create something extraordinary together.
